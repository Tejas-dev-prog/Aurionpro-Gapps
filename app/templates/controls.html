New Control

Create a new control which can be added to your projects. Subcontrols are child tasks of the control. For example, if you create a control with two (2) subcontrols, you would complete the subcontrols and the top level control would be satisfied. Subcontrols are not mandatory to use, but are highly encouraged to break down a control.
